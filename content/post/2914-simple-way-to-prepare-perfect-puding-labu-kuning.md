<h3>The ingredients needed to make Puding Labu Kuning:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Take 200 gr of labu kuning di kukus(haluskan pakai garpu). </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 300 ml of air matang. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 5 gr of agar2 plain. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 5 gr of nutrijell plain. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready  of Gula secukupny sesuai selera aja ya. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/4 sdt of garam. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/4 sdt of vanilli. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 sachet of santan instan kara@65ml. </li>
	
</ol>

<h3>Steps to make Puding Labu Kuning:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Masukkan gula + agar2 + nutrijel aduk rata.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Tambahkan labu yg sdh di haluskan, aduk.
			
			
		</li>

			Tambahkan santan + garam + vanilli + air matang.
			
			
		</li>

		</li>

		</li>
