<ol>
	
		<li>{Take 500 gr of labu kuning. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 bungkus of agar-agar plain. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 bungkus of santan instan. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 250 gr of gula pasir. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 3 1/2-4 gelas of air (disesuaikan). </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready Sejumput of garam. </li>
	
		<li>{Get Secukupnya of vanili (opsional). </li>
