<h3>The ingredients needed to make Choux Pastry:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Take 300 ml of air. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 125 gr of butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 150 gr of tepung terigu. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/4 of garam. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 5 butir of telur ukuran sedang. </li>
