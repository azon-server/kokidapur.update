		<li>{Make ready 10 sdm of cembung gula pasir. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 butir of telur. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/2 sdm of SP. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 sdt of vanili cair/bubuk. </li>

<h3>Steps to make Roti Kukus Tridatu Mekar:</h3>

<ol>

		</li>
