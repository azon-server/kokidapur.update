<h3>The ingredients needed to make Puding Labu Kuning / Pumpkin:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare 700 gr of pumpkin / labu kuning. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 3 bks of santan kara 65 ml. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 300 gr of gula pasir. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 850 ml of air. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/2 sdt of garam. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 5 bks of susu kental manis putih. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 bks of agar-agar putih (saya pakai swallow). </li>
