<h3>Instructions to make 🎂 Butter Cream Lembut simple 🎂:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Bikin sirup gula terlebih dahulu. Caranya: masak 50ml air + 5 sdm gula pasir sampai larut, dinginkan.
			
			
		</li>
