<h3>Instructions to make Bolu jagung magic com (no mixer):</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Kocak telur, gula, garam dan baking powder sampai berbuih..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Campur jagung parut dgn kotoran telur, aduk rata..
			
			
		</li>
