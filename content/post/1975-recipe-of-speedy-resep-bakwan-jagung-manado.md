<ol>

		<li>{Prepare 2 butir of telur ayam. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 bungkus of Kobe Tepung Bakwan Kress (75gr). </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 50 gram of daun bawang. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready secukupnya of minyak goreng. </li>
